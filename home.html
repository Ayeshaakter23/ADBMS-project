Add Students
Register students via the modal form — data is instantly persisted to the SQLite database through the Flask API.

Enter Grades
Use the Grade Entry panel to input scores for all 6 subjects. Averages and letter grades are auto-calculated server-side.
03

Explore the Analytics and Attendance modules to identify patterns, flag at-risk students, and compare class performance.
04
Export Reports
Generate merit lists, at-risk reports, and official transcripts, then export them as PDF for administration or parents.
